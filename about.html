Our Story

Buda Fence Company was founded in 2015 by William Espich, a Buda native with a passion for quality craftsmanship. What started as a one-man operation has grown into a trusted team serving Central Texas.

Over the years, we've completed more than 5,000 fence installations and built a reputation for clean, consistent work—done right the first time. We’re based in Buda, TX and proudly serve homeowners across Buda, Austin, Kyle, and Dripping Springs.

We specialize in projects that improve curb appeal and property value—like cedar privacy fences, clean and durable metal fencing, and reliable fence repairs when your fence is leaning, rotting, or failing.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions we hear from homeowners across Buda, Austin, Kyle, and Dripping Springs.

Do you need a license to build fences in Texas?

Texas does not require a state license for fence contractors. We protect homeowners with a $1,000,000 liability insurance policy and a 5-year workmanship warranty on fence installations.

What does your 5-year workmanship warranty cover?

Our 5-year workmanship warranty applies to our fence installations and covers installation-related issues under normal use. If something isn’t right due to workmanship, we’ll make it right.

(If you want, we can add a short “Warranty Details” page later for even more trust and SEO.)

How long does a typical fence install take?

Many standard residential fence projects can be completed in a day, depending on linear footage, terrain, access, and material selections. We’ll give you a clear timeline before we start.

Do you handle fence repairs and replacements?

Yes. We handle fence repairs and full replacements, including sagging gates, rotted posts, leaning fence lines, and storm damage. We’ll recommend the most cost-effective solution for your property.

Which areas do you serve?

We’re based in Buda, Texas and proudly serve nearby communities including Austin, Kyle, and Dripping Springs.
