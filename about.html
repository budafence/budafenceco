Our Story

Founded in 2015, Buda Fence Company began with a simple mission: to provide Central Texas homeowners with high-quality fencing solutions that combine durability, beauty, and value. What started as a small family business has grown into one of the most trusted fence installation companies in the Austin area.

Over the years, we've completed more than 5,000 fence installations, earning a reputation for exceptional craftsmanship, reliable service, and customer satisfaction. Our commitment to quality has made us the go-to choice for homeowners throughout Buda, Austin, Kyle, and Dripping Springs.

We're a small family business located right here in Buda, and we strive to provide exceptional service to our community. We build relationships that last through professional, honest, and reliable services at a fair price.

Meet the Owner

William Espich with his family — the heart behind Buda Fence Company

William Espich

Founder & Owner

With over 15 years in the fencing industry, William founded Buda Fence Company in 2015 to bring quality craftsmanship to Central Texas. His hands-on approach means he's involved in every project, ensuring the same high standards that built the company's reputation.

As a Buda resident and family man, William understands what homeowners need: a fence that's built right, looks great, and lasts for years. He takes pride in treating every customer's property like his own.

When he's not building fences, William enjoys spending time with his family and being an active member of the Buda community.
