Decking Materials That Perform in Central Texas

Composite Decking (Trex®, TimberTech®)

Best for homeowners who want the “set it and forget it” option — low maintenance, consistent look, and excellent longevity.

  • Low maintenance: no sanding/staining cycles
  • Durable: resists rot, insects, and moisture issues
  • Clean aesthetics: modern colors and finishes

Wood Decks (Cedar, Pressure-Treated Pine, Ipe)

Real wood is timeless. We’ll match your goals — budget-friendly, natural beauty, or premium hardwood longevity.

  • Cedar: beautiful grain, classic look, great performance with proper maintenance
  • Pressure-treated pine: cost-effective and strong
  • Ipe hardwood: premium durability and rich color

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best decking material for the Texas climate?

Composite is low maintenance and stable. Ipe is premium durability. Cedar is a classic option that performs well when properly maintained.

Do I need a permit to build a deck in Austin, TX?

Often yes for elevated/attached/covered decks. Requirements vary by city and project details. We’ll guide you through what applies to your build.

How much does a new deck cost in Austin?

It depends on size, height, and material choice. We provide a clear, itemized estimate so you know exactly what you’re paying for.
