When to Repair vs. Replace Your Fence

Here’s our straight answer based on what we see every week in Central Texas:

✓ Repair Makes Sense When:

  • • Less than 25% of fence is damaged
  • • Posts and framework are still solid
  • • Damage is localized to one area
  • • Only a few pickets/panels need replacing
  • • Gate hardware needs adjustment or replacement

✗ Replacement May Be Better When:

  • • More than 25% of fence is damaged
  • • Multiple posts are rotted or broken
  • • Widespread rot or insect damage
  • • Fence is leaning in multiple sections
  • • Repair cost approaches replacement cost

We’ll always give you an honest recommendation. If repair makes sense, we’ll repair it. If replacement is the better long-term investment, we’ll tell you that too.
