Frequently Asked Questions About Metal Fences

How long does a metal fence last in Texas?

A professionally installed metal fence can last 30-50 years or more in the Central Texas climate. Aluminum and powder-coated steel are virtually maintenance-free and won't rust, rot, or warp. Many manufacturers offer lifetime warranties on the materials, and we provide a 5-year installation warranty on all our work.

What's the difference between wrought iron and aluminum fencing?

Wrought iron is heavier, stronger, and more expensive. It requires periodic maintenance to prevent rust. Aluminum is lighter, rust-proof, and maintenance-free. Modern aluminum fences can look identical to wrought iron at a fraction of the cost and weight. For most residential applications, aluminum provides the perfect balance of beauty, durability, and value.

Are metal fences safe for pools in Austin?

Yes! We install pool fences that meet all Austin and Texas pool code requirements, including proper height (minimum 4 feet), self-closing hinges, and child-safety latches positioned out of reach. Our pool fences provide security without obstructing your view, and they're designed to keep children safe while enhancing your outdoor living space.

Can metal fences be installed on slopes or retaining walls?

Absolutely! We specialize in challenging terrain. Our wrackable panels adjust to follow grade changes naturally, and we have extensive experience installing metal fences on retaining walls, hillsides, and uneven ground throughout the Austin Hill Country. Where other fence types would look awkward or require extensive custom work, metal fences flow gracefully with the landscape.

Do you offer custom colors for metal fences?

Yes! While black is our most popular color, we offer custom powder-coated finishes in virtually any color you desire. Powder coating provides superior durability and rust resistance compared to paint. Popular options include bronze, white, green, and custom colors to match your home's exterior.

How long does metal fence installation take?

Most residential metal fence projects are completed in one day, similar to wood fence installations. Larger properties or complex terrain may require an additional day. We'll provide a clear timeline during your consultation and keep you informed throughout the process.

Do metal fences require maintenance?

Aluminum and powder-coated steel fences require virtually no maintenance‚Äîjust occasional cleaning with soap and water. Wrought iron may require periodic inspection and touch-up painting every 5-10 years. All our metal fences feature rust-resistant coatings that protect against the elements for decades.
