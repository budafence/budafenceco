Why Choose a Metal Fence for Your Austin Property?

In Central Texas, where extreme weather and intense sun can take a toll on fencing materials, metal fences offer unparalleled advantages that make them the smart choice for homeowners and businesses alike.

Unmatched Durability and Longevity

A professionally installed metal fence can last 30-50 years or more with minimal maintenance. Unlike wood that rots, warps, or falls victim to termites, metal fences stand strong through decades of Texas heat, storms, and humidity. Our aluminum and powder-coated steel options are virtually maintenance-free‚Äîno painting, staining, or sealing required.

Superior Security Without Sacrificing Style

Metal fences provide exceptional security while maintaining an open, elegant appearance. The strength of steel or wrought iron creates a formidable barrier that deters intruders, while the vertical picket design allows visibility and doesn't make your property feel closed off. This makes metal fencing ideal for front yards, pool enclosures, and commercial properties where both security and aesthetics matter.

Perfect for Pool Safety and Code Compliance

Austin and Texas have strict pool fencing requirements designed to protect children. Metal fences are the preferred choice for pool enclosures because they meet all safety codes while providing unobstructed views of your pool area. We install self-closing hinges, child-safety latches positioned out of reach, and ensure proper height requirements are met‚Äîall while creating a beautiful frame for your outdoor oasis.

Adaptable to Any Terrain

The Austin area is known for challenging terrain‚Äîrocky hillsides, retaining walls, and significant grade changes. Metal fences excel in these conditions. Our wrackable panel systems adjust naturally to follow slopes, and we have extensive experience installing metal fences on retaining walls and uneven ground throughout the Hill Country. Where wood fences would require extensive custom cutting and still look awkward, metal fences flow gracefully with the landscape.

Rust-Resistant and Weather-Proof

Our metal fences feature advanced rust-resistant coatings that protect against the elements. Aluminum naturally resists corrosion, while our steel and wrought iron options receive multi-layer powder coating that creates an impenetrable barrier against moisture and oxidation. This means your fence will look as good in 20 years as it does on installation day.

Types of Metal Fences We Install

We offer a comprehensive range of metal fencing options to suit any property style, budget, and functional requirement. Here's a detailed look at your choices:

Aluminum Fencing

The most popular choice for residential properties, aluminum fencing offers the classic look of wrought iron at a fraction of the weight and cost. It's rust-proof, maintenance-free, and available in dozens of styles and colors.

Best For: Pool enclosures, front yard decorative fencing, perimeter security

Heights Available: 3ft, 4ft, 5ft, 6ft

Wrought Iron Fencing

The traditional choice for those seeking maximum strength and a classic, timeless appearance. Wrought iron is heavier and stronger than aluminum, making it ideal for high-security applications and grand estate entrances.

Best For: Estate fencing, commercial security, decorative driveway gates

Maintenance: Requires periodic inspection and touch-up painting

Steel Fencing

Industrial-strength fencing with powder-coated finish for maximum durability. Steel fencing provides the highest level of security while maintaining an attractive appearance.

Best For: Commercial properties, high-security residential, industrial facilities

Features: Heaviest gauge material, maximum strength

Ornamental Metal Fencing

Decorative fencing featuring scrollwork, finials, and custom designs. Perfect for adding elegance and curb appeal to your property while maintaining security.

Best For: Front yards, garden enclosures, decorative accents

Customization: Endless design possibilities

Puppy Panel Metal Fencing

Standard metal fencing with additional horizontal rails at the bottom to prevent small dogs from escaping. The tighter spacing keeps puppies and small pets safely contained.

Best For: Dog owners, pet containment, child safety

Features: Closer picket spacing at ground level

Our Professional Metal Fence Installation Process

A metal fence is only as good as its installation. We've perfected our process over thousands of installations to ensure your fence is structurally sound, perfectly level, and built to last decades.

1

Free On-Site Consultation & Detailed Quote

We begin with a thorough site visit to understand your needs, measure your property, and assess any terrain challenges. We'll discuss style options, height requirements, and any specific concerns like pool codes or HOA regulations. You'll receive a detailed, transparent quote with no hidden fees.

2

Design Finalization & Utility Line Location

Once you approve the quote, we finalize all design details including gate locations, color selection, and any custom features. We contact 811 to have all underground utilities marked, ensuring safe installation and preventing costly damage to buried lines.

3

Professional Installation with Precision Engineering

Our experienced crew sets posts to proper depth in concrete footings, ensuring a rock-solid foundation. We use laser levels to ensure perfect alignment and spacing. Posts are plumbed and braced, then allowed to cure before panels are installed. For challenging terrain, we utilize wrackable panels and custom solutions to achieve a flawless installation.

4

Gate Installation & Hardware

Gates are hung with heavy-duty hinges and equipped with your choice of locking mechanisms. For pool fences, we install self-closing hinges and child-safety latches that meet all code requirements. Every gate is tested multiple times to ensure smooth operation.

5

Final Inspection & Site Cleanup

We conduct a thorough walk-through with you to ensure every detail meets your expectations. We test all gates, check alignment, and make any final adjustments. Our crew then completely cleans the job site, removing all debris and old fencing materials, leaving your property looking immaculate.

Frequently Asked Questions About Metal Fences

How long does a metal fence last in Texas?

A professionally installed metal fence can last 30-50 years or more in the Central Texas climate. Aluminum and powder-coated steel are virtually maintenance-free and won't rust, rot, or warp. Many manufacturers offer lifetime warranties on the materials, and we provide a 5-year installation warranty on all our work.

What's the difference between wrought iron and aluminum fencing?

Wrought iron is heavier, stronger, and more expensive. It requires periodic maintenance to prevent rust. Aluminum is lighter, rust-proof, and maintenance-free. Modern aluminum fences can look identical to wrought iron at a fraction of the cost and weight. For most residential applications, aluminum provides the perfect balance of beauty, durability, and value.

Are metal fences safe for pools in Austin?

Yes! We install pool fences that meet all Austin and Texas pool code requirements, including proper height (minimum 4 feet), self-closing hinges, and child-safety latches positioned out of reach. Our pool fences provide security without obstructing your view, and they're designed to keep children safe while enhancing your outdoor living space.

Can metal fences be installed on slopes or retaining walls?

Absolutely! We specialize in challenging terrain. Our wrackable panels adjust to follow grade changes naturally, and we have extensive experience installing metal fences on retaining walls, hillsides, and uneven ground throughout the Austin Hill Country. Where other fence types would look awkward or require extensive custom work, metal fences flow gracefully with the landscape.

Do you offer custom colors for metal fences?

Yes! While black is our most popular color, we offer custom powder-coated finishes in virtually any color you desire. Powder coating provides superior durability and rust resistance compared to paint. Popular options include bronze, white, green, and custom colors to match your home's exterior.

How long does metal fence installation take?

Most residential metal fence projects are completed in one day, similar to wood fence installations. Larger properties or complex terrain may require an additional day. We'll provide a clear timeline during your consultation and keep you informed throughout the process.

Do metal fences require maintenance?

Aluminum and powder-coated steel fences require virtually no maintenance‚Äîjust occasional cleaning with soap and water. Wrought iron may require periodic inspection and touch-up painting every 5-10 years. All our metal fences feature rust-resistant coatings that protect against the elements for decades.
